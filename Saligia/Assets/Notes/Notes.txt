Lust (Luxuria) - represented by the colour blue and symbolized by the cow and the snake.

Gluttony (Gula) - represented by the colour orange and symbolized by the pig.

Greed (Avaritia) - represented by the colour yellow and symbolized by the frog.

Sloth (Acedia) - represented by the colour light blue and symbolized by the goat.

Wrath (Ira) - represented by the colour red and symbolized by the bear.

Envy (Invidia) - represented by the colour green and symbolized by the dog.

Pride (Superbia) - represented by the colour purple and symbolized by three animals: the lion, the peafowl, and the horse.
